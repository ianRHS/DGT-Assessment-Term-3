Destination Fiordland

Introduction

Fiordland, located in the soutwestern
corner of South island of New Zealand,
is home of famous Milford and Doubtful Sounds,
as well as many world famous walking tracks.
Fiordland is one of the most dramatic and beautiful
parts of New Zealand and is renowned as a sightseeing
and walking capital.
Activities and accommodation are centred on four main areas of Fiordland:
Te Anau, Manapouri, Milford sound, and Doubtful Sound.

Activities

Visitors in Fiordland have a wide range
of attractions to choose from, including all of the following activities:

  • Scenic Walks
  • Fishing
  • Boat Trips
  • Scenic Flights

    • Other activities include:
  • Diving
  • Caves
  • Golf
  • Farm Visits
  • Horse Treks

    • Some of these activities will be explained
    in person, at Fiordland for safety reasons.
